About Us

Rise and Removal is a professional junk removal company committed to helping our community get rid of unwanted items in an environmentally responsible way. Founded in 2020, we have been serving customers with reliable and efficient service.

Our team is dedicated to providing top-notch customer service, ensuring that every job is done right the first time. We believe in giving back to the community by recycling and donating items whenever possible.
